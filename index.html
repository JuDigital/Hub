The Woman Behind the Work

Who Is Ju?

Ju has been spiritually gifted since early childhood, sensing spirits, experiencing paranormal activity, and receiving things she had no language for yet. What once brought fear eventually became her greatest calling. She has been working with clients professionally since 2020 but the gifts go back much further. Today she is not just doing readings. She is training mediums and psychics in development, helping them understand their gifts for peace of mind and to build their own practice. She holds two master degrees, is a proud member of Alpha Kappa Alpha Sorority, Incorporated, and has built everything she has through real inner work, real alignment, and real sacrifice.

From a young age Ju was experiencing things others around her could not explain. Seeing spirits. Paranormal activity that no one could make sense of. Feeling things shift in a room before anything was said. It was not always comfortable. For a long time it was frightening. She was raised with a deep foundation of faith and over time came to understand that honoring her ancestors was also part of her path. Both have always been true at the same time.

She did not wait until everything was easy to build. She worked overnight shifts while in school full time, barely sleeping, writing her desires in present tense every single morning before she even knew what scripting was. She manifested her way back to Houston, the city she had always dreamed of, and built her life there from scratch.

In 2020 she launched her business officially. She created three original oracle decks from real spiritual downloads and lived experience. Her YouTube channel grew because people could feel that what she was doing was real. She was not performing. She was connecting.

Since then she has mentored thousands of students, led a spiritual community active since 2020, and built a coaching practice that now trains mediums and psychics in development. She does not just teach people how to manifest. She teaches them how to become who they were always meant to be.
